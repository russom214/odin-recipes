Bakaliaros Scordalia

Description

On the 25th of March, Greeks have a double celebration. We celebrate the Greek Independence and The Annunciation (Evangelismos) of Virgin Mary. The traditional dish of this day is Bakaliaros skordalia

Ingredients

  • Oil for frying
  • Beer
  • Egg
  • All purpose flour
  • Parsley

Instructions

  1. In a bowl place the flour, parsley, beer and mix. Add the water gradually until the batter is thick. With a spoon mix in the meringue. Refrigerate for at least an hour before battering fish.
  2. Mix cod with batter and put it in hot oil
  3. Spoon the battered cod mix and fry in batches until golden brown.
