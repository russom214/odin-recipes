Keftadakia

Description

Keftedia is one of the staples of traditional greek cousine.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Flour for coating
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Oregano
  • Parsley
  • Mint

Instructions

  1. Mix the ground beef, garlic, onion, oragano, parsley and mint.
  2. Mix them into ball sizes
  3. Fry or bake them into the oven
